About Us

The name Underwood Brand Development Company, Inc. is a word-play based on the ficticous real estate company the "Pocono Land Development Company" appearing in Harry Chapin's "Country Dreams". Country Dreams is from Harry's 1978 Dance Band on the Titanic album. David Underwood is the President. David Underwood is a moving and storage industry veteran since 1978.
